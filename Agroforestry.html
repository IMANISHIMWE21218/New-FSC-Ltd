Agroforestry And Silviculture Service

Agroforestry and Silviculture

FSC Ltd specializes in agroforestry and silviculture services, combining agricultural and forestry techniques to create more sustainable and productive land use systems. With over 15 years of experience, FSC Ltd has successfully implemented large-scale projects across Rwanda, including the production and planting of millions of tree seedlings, forest restoration, and agroforestry development. Their expertise spans seed procurement, nursery management, site preparation, planting, and training local communities in sustainable forestry practices.

Benefits of Service

  • 1 Increased land productivity through integrated farming and forestry
  • 2 Soil conservation and erosion control
  • 3Enhanced biodiversity and ecosystem services
  • 4 Carbon sequestration and climate change mitigation
  • 5 Sustainable source of timber, fuelwood, and non-timber forest products

FSC Ltd's agroforestry and silviculture services offer a holistic approach to land management, balancing agricultural production with environmental conservation. By integrating trees into farming systems, clients can improve soil fertility, diversify income sources, and contribute to climate resilience while maintaining productive agricultural landscapes.

Why Choose This Service

FSC Ltd brings extensive experience in coordinating large-scale forestry projects across Rwanda. Their team has successfully managed the production and planting of millions of tree seedlings, working closely with local communities and government agencies. With a strong focus on sustainability and gender inclusion, FSC Ltd ensures that their agroforestry and silviculture services not only improve land productivity but also address critical environmental and social issues. Their proven track record in project management, combined with technical expertise in forestry and agroforestry, makes FSC Ltd a reliable partner for implementing sustainable land use practices.
