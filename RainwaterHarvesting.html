Rainwater Harvesting Service

Rainwater Harvesting

In response to Rwanda's recurrent challenges of water scarcity, exacerbated by unpredictable rainfall patterns and increasing demand due to agricultural needs, FSC Ltd champions rainwater harvesting as a pivotal solution. This method involves the collection and storage of rainwater from rooftops, surfaces, or local catchment areas, which is then utilized for various purposes, primarily irrigation and household needs. By harnessing rainwater, FSC Ltd aims to bolster agricultural resilience and alleviate pressure on existing water sources, thereby fostering sustainable development and enhancing community livelihoods.

Benefits of Service

  • 1 Conservation of water resources
  • 2 Reduction of dependency on mains water supply
  • 3 Mitigation of stormwater runoff and erosion
  • 4 Sustainable and cost-effective water supply solution for agriculture, households, and communities.

Rainwater harvesting by FSC Ltd not only addresses immediate water needs but also contributes to long-term environmental sustainability and community resilience. Our proven track record and commitment to excellence make us the ideal partner for implementing effective water management solutions in Rwanda.
