About us

About us

Driving Sustainable Development and Setting Benchmarks for Excellence

FSC Ltd, formerly TWUNGANIRANE association, established in 1998 and rebranded in 2013, is dedicated to farming, silvicultural activities, and environmental protection in Rwanda. Registered with Rwanda Development Board under number 102953287, it specializes in forestry and agroforestry, focusing on sustainability and community empowerment.

The company has planted 2,432 hectares of forests, sequestering 250,496 tons of carbon. It collaborates with RHODA, PAFOR Project, DUHAMIC, and others, contributing to biodiversity and sustainable land management. FSC Ltd promotes gender equality with women in key roles and empowers communities through agroforestry training.
