Our Mission

Through PAEMA, we aim to create a thriving agricultural ecosystem that integrates modern farming techniques with eco-friendly practices, fostering economic growth, food security, and sustainability. We strive to equip farmers with the knowledge, resources, and market access they need to build profitable and resilient farming ventures.

What We Do

Agricultural Extension Services

We provide comprehensive training in crop management, livestock care, and sustainable farming practices.

Livestock Support & Animal Husbandry

Our expert veterinary care and breeding programs improve livestock health and product quality.

Agri-Business Training

We equip farmers with business management skills, including financial literacy and marketing.

Sustainability at the Core

We promote agroecological practices, water conservation, and climate-smart farming techniques.

Our Impact

Since the inception of PAEMA, we have seen tangible improvements in the livelihoods of hundreds of farmers in Mageragere. With enhanced access to resources, training, and markets, farmers are now producing more, with better quality, and at more competitive prices.

  • Higher crop yields
  • Improved livestock health
  • Stronger community engagement
